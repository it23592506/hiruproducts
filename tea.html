About Our Ceylon Tea

Our Premium Ceylon Tea is carefully selected from the finest tea gardens of Sri Lanka, where the combination of ideal climate, altitude, and soil creates the perfect conditions for exceptional tea cultivation.

Each leaf is handpicked at the optimal time to ensure maximum flavor and aroma. Our traditional processing methods preserve the natural antioxidants and create the distinctive taste that Ceylon tea is renowned for worldwide.

Perfect Brewing Guide

1

Water Temperature

Heat water to 195-205°F (90-96°C)

2

Tea Amount

Use 1 teaspoon per cup (250ml)

3

Steeping Time

Steep for 3-5 minutes for optimal flavor

4

Enjoy

Serve hot or add ice for refreshing iced tea
