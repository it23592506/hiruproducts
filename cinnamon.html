About Our Ceylon Cinnamon

Ceylon Cinnamon, known as "True Cinnamon," is exclusively grown in Sri Lanka and is considered the finest cinnamon in the world. Unlike common cassia cinnamon, Ceylon cinnamon has a sweet, delicate flavor with subtle citrusy notes.

Our cinnamon is harvested from the inner bark of Cinnamomum verum trees, carefully processed using traditional methods passed down through generations. The result is cinnamon quills with superior quality, aroma, and therapeutic properties.
